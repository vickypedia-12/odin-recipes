Crunchy Ramen Broccoli Slaw

This broccoli slaw recipe was passed arounf for years in my grandmother's beauty salon. She took it to every srunch supper. It was on the table every Thanksgiving and Christmas.



Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time:

10 mins

Total time:

25 mins

servings:

8


Ingredients

  • 1/4cup butter
  • 2 packages Oriental-flavor ramen noodle soup, seasoning packet reserved
  • 1/2 cup slivered almonds
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 1 package broccoli coleslaw mix
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion

Dressing:

  • 1/2 cup salad oil
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 table-spoons of soy sauce

Directions

step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).


step 2

melt butter in a microwave -safe bowl in the microwave. Crush ramen noodles small pieces; stir crushed noodles, almonds, and sesame seeds into melted butter. Spread mixture onto a baking sheet.


step 3

bake in the preheated oven until golden brown adn crunchy, 8 to 10 minutes; allow to cool completely.


step 4

Toss together coleslaw mix and green onion in a large serving bowl.


step 5

Make dressing: Whisk together oil, sugar,vinegar,soy sauce, and reserved seasoning packets in a separate bowl until well combined.


step 6

Stir cooled noodle mixture into dressing. Pour dressing over coleslaw-onion mixture; toss to coat.
